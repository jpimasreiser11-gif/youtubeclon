# Comparative Analysis: Opus Clips vs. Munch vs. Submagic

This document synthesizes features and technical capabilities of leading AI video clipping platforms to inform improvements for our EduMind AI Learning Guide (OpusClips Clone).

## 1. Opus Clips (The Standard)
*   **ClipAnything Model**: Optimized for diverse content beyond just podcasts (vlogs, sports, gaming).
*   **Virality Score**: Uses AI to predict success (0-100) based on hook quality and retention patterns.
*   **Active Speaker Detection**: Auto-reframing to keep the speaker centered in vertical format.
*   **Document-style Editing**: Edit video by editing the transcript (text-based trimming).
- **AI B-Roll**: Automatically adds relevant B-roll footage or AI-generated images based on the context of the speech.

## 2. Munch (The Strategist)
*   **Social Trend Integration**: Cross-references clip content with current social media trends (TikTok, IG Trends).
*   **Keyword Analytics**: Suggests keywords for tags and descriptions based on virality potential.
*   **Multi-Platform Optimization**: Different edits for LinkedIn vs. TikTok (different professional/casual styles).

## 3. Submagic (The Retention Expert)
*   **Dynamic Captions**: Captions with emojis, bolded keywords, and animations (similar to Alex Hormozi style).
*   **Magic Trimming**: Intelligent silence removal and "dead air" detection.
*   **Translation**: Multi-language support for international audiences with voice cloning.
