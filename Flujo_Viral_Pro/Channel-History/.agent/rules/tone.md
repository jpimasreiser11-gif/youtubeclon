# History Channel Tone Rules

- **Voice Profile**: History (Pitch -2Hz, academic British accent).
- **Audio**: Orchestral marches, period-specific pieces, epic soundtracks.
- **Narrative**: Academic but engaging. Linear chronology. Cite primary sources. Use "Storytelling" to humanize historical figures.
- **Visuals**: Primary source documents, archival sepia footage, maps, 3D Ken Burns on paintings.
- **Pacing**: Evolutionary, steady rhythm, informative peaks.
