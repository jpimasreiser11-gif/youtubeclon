# YouTube Script Template - Mind Warp

## Inputs
- Core claim:
- Research references to validate:
- Daily-life scenario:
- Viewer transformation target:

## Structure (8-14 min)
1. **HOOK (0:00-0:12)**
   - Contrarian statement + immediate implication.
2. **PROMISE (0:12-0:35)**
   - What viewers will decode about their own behavior.
3. **CASE + CONCEPT BLOCKS**
   - Real-world scenario.
   - Mechanism (bias, loop, framing, etc.).
   - Viewer application.
   - End with open loop.
4. **CTA @ ~85%**
   - Invite to next related deep-dive.
5. **CLOSING**
   - One unsettling but actionable takeaway.

## Retention rules
- New hook every 30-45s.
- Use contrast phrases: "You think X, but evidence shows Y".
- Add pattern interrupts (question, visual metaphor, mini-story).

## Anti-ban / Safety
- Don’t present diagnosis or therapy claims.
- Avoid encouraging manipulation or harm.
- Frame tactics as awareness and self-protection.
