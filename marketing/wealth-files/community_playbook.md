# Community Playbook - Wealth Files

## Objective
Turn comments into useful discussion, stronger retention, and repeat-viewer signals for Wealth Files.

## Recommended response SLA
- First pass: key comments in the first 2 hours.
- Second pass: new threads answered within 24h.
- Daily moderation: remove spam, abuse, and suspicious links.

## Comment types and response style
1. **Good-faith question**: Concrete answer + timestamp when relevant.
2. **Constructive skepticism**: Validate concern and cite source/context.
3. **High-value contribution**: Heart + thank + ask permission to feature.
4. **Aggressive criticism**: One short reply only if useful; otherwise stop escalation.

## Fast reply templates
- "Great question. At {TIMESTAMP} we break down {POINT}. Want a deeper follow-up on {SUBTOPIC}?"
- "Fair pushback. Main source: {SOURCE}; also see {SOURCE_2}."
- "Excellent addition. Can we feature your point in the next upload?"

## Weekly engagement tactics
- Publish 2 community prompts linked to latest upload.
- Feature 1 top comment in the next video intro.
- End each video with a specific debate CTA.

## Guardrails
- No unverified promises or unsupported claims.
- No personal arguments.
- Prioritize clarity, respect, and usefulness.
