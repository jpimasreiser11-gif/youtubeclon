# mind-warp

Use for English psychology and behavior-science content for Mind Warp.

## Voice
- Authoritative but accessible.
- Experiment-backed claims, unsettling implications.

## Title formulas
- "[Counterintuitive truth] + [daily life implication]"
- "What your brain does when [common behavior]"

## Hooks
- Your brain is lying to you right now.
- A classic experiment proved this.
- You make this mistake without noticing.

## Script structure
1. Claim
2. Evidence
3. Real-world implication
4. Retention hook
5. Reflective close
