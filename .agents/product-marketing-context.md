# Product Marketing Context - YouTube Automation Pro

## Positioning
YouTube Automation Pro is a multi-channel video production system that turns validated trends into publish-ready videos with consistent brand voice.

## Channel-as-product mapping

### 1) Impacto Mundial
- Audience: Spanish-speaking mystery/history viewers (18-45)
- Promise: forbidden historical truths with cinematic tension
- Viral definition: high watch-time on long-form mystery + above-average comments
- Value moat: archival framing + dramatic narrative pacing

### 2) Mentes Rotas
- Audience: true-crime and criminal-psychology viewers (20-50)
- Promise: case reconstruction with investigative structure
- Viral definition: high retention beyond minute 5 + saves/shares
- Value moat: evidence-first structure, no tabloid style

### 3) El Loco de la IA
- Audience: Spanish-speaking builders/solopreneurs (18-40)
- Promise: practical AI workflows with immediate ROI
- Viral definition: CTR + click-to-action conversion to tools
- Value moat: implementation-focused scripts, real time/cost numbers

### 4) Mind Warp
- Audience: EN psychology/cognition enthusiasts (18-45)
- Promise: unsettling but scientific behavior insights
- Viral definition: strong 30-second retention and rewatch
- Value moat: study-backed writing + narrative implications

### 5) Wealth Files
- Audience: EN aspirational finance audience (22-50)
- Promise: hidden wealth mechanics and case breakdowns
- Viral definition: high CTR + long watch-time on 12-20 min videos
- Value moat: number-driven storytelling, anti-generic advice

### 6) Dark Science
- Audience: EN science curiosity segment (16-40)
- Promise: cosmic/deep-science explained with cinematic awe
- Viral definition: high completion + high comment depth
- Value moat: scale analogies + documentary cadence

## Voice and tone guide (global)
- Direct and specific, no generic AI phrasing.
- Every intro starts with a concrete high-impact claim.
- No filler openings or corporate language.

## Success metrics
- CTR by channel
- 30-second retention and average view duration
- Publish consistency vs schedule
- Error-free pipeline completion rate
- Revenue per 1k views by niche
