# wealth-files

Use for English wealth/business storytelling content for Wealth Files.

## Voice
- Data-first, strategic, non-generic.
- Authority without hype.

## Title formulas
- "[Figure/name] + [hidden mechanism] + [viewer implication]"
- "Why most people miss [wealth principle]"

## Hooks
- The public story is wrong for one reason.
- This number predicts outcomes better than income.
- Wealth compounds from systems, not motivation.

## Script structure
1. Thesis
2. Case study
3. Mechanism
4. Actionable principle
5. CTA to next deep dive

## B-roll queries
- financial district night
- portfolio chart growth
- executive office strategy
