# SEO Strategy - Dark Science

## Channel SEO objective
- Own search intent around deep science, cosmic scale and unresolved anomalies.
- Match title/description/tags to how the English-speaking science curiosity viewers searches on YouTube.
- Favor informational + narrative intent, avoid empty hype wording.

## Main YouTube keywords (core)
- space documentary
- science mysteries
- cosmic anomalies
- astrophysics explained
- deep ocean science
- particle physics
- unexplained signals
- future of science
- scientific discoveries
- universe secrets

## Long-tail keywords (specific intent)
- cosmic anomalies scientists still cannot explain
- astrophysics concepts explained with simple analogies
- deep ocean discoveries and why they matter
- science mysteries based on real data
- particle physics breakthroughs for non-scientists
- unexplained space signals and current theories
- documentary style science storytelling
- what new telescopes reveal about the universe
- scientific uncertainty and competing hypotheses
- how scale in space changes human perspective

## Weekly execution (actionable)
1. Pick 1 core keyword + 2 long-tail keywords per video.
2. Put the core keyword near the start of the title and first description line.
3. Repeat semantic variations in intro, summary, and CTA blocks.
4. Add 5-8 tags: 2 core, 3 long-tail, 1-3 timely/context tags.
5. Review CTR/retention at 72h and update title/thumbnail when needed.

## Per-video checklist
- [ ] Primary keyword appears naturally in title.
- [ ] First description line contains promise + keyword.
- [ ] 2-3 long-tail terms used without stuffing.
- [ ] Closing hashtags match topic and audience intent.
