## Affiliate integration blocks (ready placeholders)
- `{AFFILIATE_BLOCK_1}`: Primary product used in the episode.
- `{AFFILIATE_BLOCK_2}`: Lower-cost alternative or free trial.
- `{AFFILIATE_BLOCK_3}`: Complementary resource (template, book, course).

Suggested block format:
```md
- Resource: {RESOURCE_NAME}
- Why it helps: {DIRECT_BENEFIT}
- Link: {AFFILIATE_OR_DIRECT_URL}
```

## Operating rules
1. Lead with hook + topic in line one.
2. Cap affiliate blocks at 3 to protect trust.
3. Promote resources actually used in the video.
4. Keep disclosure line in every upload.
5. Validate links before publishing.

## Frequent resource categories for this channel
- science books, telescope gear, documentary subscriptions
