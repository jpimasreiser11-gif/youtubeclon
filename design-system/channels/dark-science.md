# Channel Design System - Dark Science

- Language: EN
- Core vibe: cosmic awe + existential tension.
- Primary palette:
  - primary `#1565C0`
  - secondary `#0A0A0A`
  - accent `#00E5FF`

## Tone and copy
- Documentary-quality narration, wonder mixed with dread.
- Explain complex ideas clearly, then widen the implication.

## Thumbnail direction
- Archetype: `deep_space`
- Keywords: deep ocean, cosmic void, anomaly highlights, scientific overlays.

## UI identity layer
- Blue/cyan for science metrics and anomaly indicators.
- Use layered depth effects for timeline/scene planning.
- Recommended icon set: telescope, atom, wave.
