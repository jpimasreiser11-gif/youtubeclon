# SEO Strategy - Mind Warp

## Channel SEO objective
- Own search intent around psychology and cognitive blind spots.
- Match title/description/tags to how the English-speaking psychology/behavior audience searches on YouTube.
- Favor informational + narrative intent, avoid empty hype wording.

## Main YouTube keywords (core)
- psychology facts
- cognitive bias
- behavior science
- how the brain works
- mental shortcuts
- decision making psychology
- social psychology
- brain experiment
- human behavior explained
- rewire your thinking

## Long-tail keywords (specific intent)
- cognitive biases that affect daily decisions
- famous psychology experiments explained simply
- why your brain makes predictable mistakes
- behavior science lessons for everyday life
- social psychology examples you miss every day
- how mental shortcuts shape your choices
- surprising brain facts backed by studies
- how to notice cognitive bias in real time
- psychology of habits and motivation
- counterintuitive behavior science insights

## Weekly execution (actionable)
1. Pick 1 core keyword + 2 long-tail keywords per video.
2. Put the core keyword near the start of the title and first description line.
3. Repeat semantic variations in intro, summary, and CTA blocks.
4. Add 5-8 tags: 2 core, 3 long-tail, 1-3 timely/context tags.
5. Review CTR/retention at 72h and update title/thumbnail when needed.

## Per-video checklist
- [ ] Primary keyword appears naturally in title.
- [ ] First description line contains promise + keyword.
- [ ] 2-3 long-tail terms used without stuffing.
- [ ] Closing hashtags match topic and audience intent.
