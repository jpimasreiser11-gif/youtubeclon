# TikTok Template - Dark Science

## Duration
30-45 seconds, one anomaly per clip.

## Sequence
1. **0-3s:** Disturbing scientific hook.
2. **3-12s:** What was measured.
3. **12-26s:** Why it is hard to explain.
4. **26-36s:** Best current interpretation.
5. **36-45s:** CTA to full science breakdown.

## Visual rhythm
- 4-5 shot changes.
- One scale comparison overlay.
- Captions with key terms only.

## Safety / Compliance
- No fake certainty.
- No "scientists confirmed" without source context.
- Avoid apocalyptic claims as definitive outcomes.
