# YouTube Script Template - Wealth Files

## Inputs
- Financial case/company/person:
- Time window:
- Key numbers (3-7):
- Main misconception to challenge:

## Structure (10-16 min)
1. **HOOK**
   - Lead with a surprising number and context.
2. **PROMISE**
   - What viewers will understand about wealth mechanics.
3. **CASE BREAKDOWN**
   - Timeline
   - Capital allocation decision
   - Consequence (wins/losses)
   - Transferable lesson
4. **CTA (Integrated)**
   - Invite viewers to next linked case study.
5. **CLOSING**
   - One principle + one caution.

## Retention
- Number reveal every ~45s.
- Contrast expected outcome vs actual outcome.
- Use mini cliffhangers: "The real risk wasn’t what investors feared..."

## Anti-ban / Compliance
- No investment advice language.
- No guaranteed returns claims.
- Add context: informational/educational only.
- Distinguish facts, assumptions, and opinion.
