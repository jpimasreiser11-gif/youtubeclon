# TikTok Template - Wealth Files

## Duration
35-50 seconds with one financial insight.

## Sequence
1. **0-3s:** Hook with a number.
2. **3-12s:** Explain what most people think.
3. **12-28s:** Show what the numbers actually show.
4. **28-40s:** Transferable lesson.
5. **40-50s:** CTA to long-form breakdown.

## On-screen text
- Card 1: Myth
- Card 2: Number
- Card 3: Mechanism
- Card 4: Lesson

## Safety
- "Not financial advice" framing.
- No unrealistic wealth promises.
