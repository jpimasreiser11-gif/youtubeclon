# Channel Design System - El Loco de la IA

- Language: ES
- Core vibe: practical AI, rapid wins, no fluff.
- Primary palette:
  - primary `#00E5FF`
  - secondary `#0A0A0A`
  - accent `#FFFFFF`

## Tone and copy
- Energetic and direct.
- Promise concrete outcomes with time anchors.

## Thumbnail direction
- Archetype: `tech_futuristic`
- Keywords: dashboards, speed lines, before/after productivity, glowing UI.

## UI identity layer
- Primary CTA glow in cyan.
- Data tables prioritize "time saved" and "ROI".
- Recommended icon set: rocket, bot, lightning.
