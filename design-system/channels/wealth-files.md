# Channel Design System - Wealth Files

- Language: EN
- Core vibe: elite money narratives, power systems, hard numbers.
- Primary palette:
  - primary `#FFD700`
  - secondary `#0A0A0A`
  - accent `#E5E4E2`

## Tone and copy
- Authoritative and data-driven.
- Use "hidden mechanism" framing rather than generic motivation.

## Thumbnail direction
- Archetype: `luxury_premium`
- Keywords: financial skyline, gold overlays, contrast-heavy portraits.

## UI identity layer
- Gold KPI accents for revenue, CPM, trend confidence.
- Premium card framing with restrained glow.
- Recommended icon set: vault, chart-up, crown.
