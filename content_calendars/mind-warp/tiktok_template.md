# TikTok Template - Mind Warp

## Duration
30-50 seconds, one bias per video.

## Sequence
1. **0-3s:** "Your brain does this without permission."
2. **3-12s:** Name the bias + simple definition.
3. **12-28s:** Daily-life example.
4. **28-40s:** Quick self-defense tactic.
5. **40-50s:** CTA to full breakdown.

## Format
- 1 sentence = 1 subtitle card.
- Use bold keyword highlights (bias name, trigger, fix).

## Safety
- No mental health diagnosis language.
- No coercive manipulation instructions.
