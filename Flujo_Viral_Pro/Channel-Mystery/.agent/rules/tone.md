# Mystery Channel Tone Rules

- **Voice Profile**: Mystery (Pitch -5Hz, Rate -10%, deep masculine).
- **Audio**: Prioritize drones, low frequencies, and dissonant tracks. High tension.
- **Narrative**: Use rhetorical questions. Maintain an air of secrecy. Focus on "the unknown" or "the macabre".
- **Visuals**: Dark imagery, high contrast, subtle fog/particle overlays.
- **Pacing**: Slower reveals followed by fast-cut hooks.
